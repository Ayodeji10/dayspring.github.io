Victor Olotu

Chief Executive Officer

Hon Victor Olotu was a former Chairman of Ose Local Government of Ondo State. Prior to his stint in public service, he had spent over 18years with Stanbic IBTC, Intercontinental Bank and Access Bank as an astute Banker/Stockbroker and an Investment Analyst with experience ranging from Accountancy, Audit, Banking, Finance, Training and Human Capital Development. Hon Olotu also headed for 3 years, the Business Development and Strategic Division of Sifax Group, a Nigerian Business Conglomerate with interests in Maritime, Aviation, Haulage & Logistics, Oil & Gas, Hospitality and Financial Services in Nigeria and in Europe and in the United States of America.

Hon Victor Olotu is a graduate of Applied Accounting from the Oxford Brookes University, United Kingdom. He is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria, an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers, Associate of the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria, a Certified Information System Auditor (CISA) of the Information System Audit and Control Association of the United States. He is an authorised dealer of The Nigerian Stock Exchange.
